Explore Some Top Localities


HINJEWADI


The top IT sector of Pune undoubtedly has to be Hinjewadi. In a very short time, the area has developed significantly. As a result, it has caught the attention of various real estate developers. Several real estate projects have come up in the past few years to cater to the increasing demand for residential real estate. Hinjewadi is located close to the city centre. If you're living in Hinjewadi, you'd strike a balance between fun and work.


VIMAN NAGAR


If you're seeking job opportunities and planning to move to Pune, Viman Nagar can be your go-to place. There are many IT parks in this place. As one of the most sought residential areas of Pune, Viman Nagar has all the facilities you will need for comfortable living. Viman Nagar is well connected to nearby localities like Kalyani Nagar, Lohegaon, Dhanori and more. Being almost close to the city centre, transportation in Viman Nagar is not a problem.


KOREGAON PARK


Undoubtedly, one of the top residential choices of Pune has to be Koregaon Park. Apart from high-value real estate, there are many commercial properties in Koregaon Park. Being located in the city centre, it will not be wrong to call it the heart of Pune. Koregaon Park brings in the perfect entertainment opportunities, ranging from the best cafes to restaurants and more. As it is in the centre, it is well connected to other parts of the city via public transport. One of the most unique features of Koregaon Park is that it offers the perfect blend of nature and modern facilities.


AUNDH


If you want peace, luxury and comfort, you should consider living in Aundh, one of the prominent high-end localities. A serene locality in Pune, Aundh is very popular among the people for its call environment. No wonder it is one of the most preferred choices for residential real estate investment. Being connected to the main parts of the city, you can easily avail public transport and visit the places. Whether you're a bachelor in Pune or a family, Aundh will always have something surprising for you.


KALYANI NAGAR


Kalyani Nagar is one of those localities where you can find everything, from commercial projects to residential ones. It is a very sought location that is close to IT Parks, shopping malls and entertainment centres. Public transport is also available here. So, travelling from Kalyani Nagar to other parts of the city will be pretty cheap. Moreover, Kalyani Nagar is very close to the Pune Junction railway station.


NIBM Road


If you want to stay close to the airport and railway station, you must consider living in NIBM Road. Because of its proximity to the railway and airport, the property prices are very high. Therefore, the rental yield will be high too. Whether you're living here as a student or working professional, NIBM Road will allow you to balance your work and life. It is undoubtedly one of the best places to live for young couples and bachelors.


SHIVAJINAGAR


Another very posh locality in Pune is Shivaji Nagar. As one of the most posh residential areas, Shivaji Nagar houses several government offices. The ongoing developments in Shivaji Nagar have caught the eyes of investors. If you're planning to invest in Pune at an affordable rate, you must consider Shivaji Nagar. It is anticipated that the region will grow manifold in the coming times.


MAGARPATTA CITY


Magarpatta City is one of the most expensive localities in Pune. There are residential areas with luxurious properties, bungalows and apartments. However, there are limited commercial properties in Magarpatta City. Magarpatta City is well-connected to hospitals, schools and colleges. There are CCTV cameras installed in the area to ensure safety across the place. These cameras are available at every intersection. Moreover, during peak hours, police conduct regular patrolling sessions to prevent the risk of theft and robbery.


KHARADI


Although located in the suburban areas of Pune, Kharadi is quite a posh region. It is located mostly around the eastern side of Pune and is known for its IT park. Apart from the huge IT parks, there are some small-scale industries too. The real estate property prices in Kharadi are pretty high because of its proximity to the airport and railway. Due to the availability of local transport, movement is pretty easy. The IT Park World Trade Centre is located in Kharadi. As a result, there are many employment opportunities in this region.
